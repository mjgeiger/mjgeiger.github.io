Academic Advising Notes

  • Advising appointments will be scheduled through my Calendly page.

    • Your meeting must be scheduled at least 24 hours in advance--the site won't let you sign up any later than that.

    • All meetings will be scheduled for 30 minutes.

    • Please pick up an advising sheet from Ball 301 and bring it to our meeting, which will be in my office (Perry 118A)

      • Students planning to graduate in the next semester must also complete a "D Form"

    • Please come to the appointment with a very good idea of what you plan to take next semester.

      • Check the Class Schedule page for the upcoming semester's course list

      • During advising in the spring, that plan should include both summer and fall courses.

    • It's completely up to you to find sections of each class that fit into your schedule and to identify time conflicts ahead of time.
